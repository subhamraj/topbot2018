About us

IIIT Allahabad, an Institute of Excellence in the field of IT has grown leaps and bounds over the past few years.It has proved its mettle not only in academics but also in the overall development of its students through various fests! Aparoksha, is the annual technical festival of IIIT- Allahabad. It is a collaboration of tech-enthusiasts to showcase their creativity through technology.It organises a plethora of events and brings an opportunity for the students coming in to participate from all around the country to prove their calibre in science and technology.


So, to all the tech-enthusiasts who want their code to walk the talk, Topbot 3.0 is here for you! Come in, code, build, innovate and grab hold of the various prizes up for grabs.

TopBot is the flagship robotics event of Aparoksha, catering to the hunger of robogeeks and challenging your robot to accomplish a particular task and reach the finish line first !Topbot gives you two captivating themes this year :

BOLT :

an obstacle avoiding contest that requires your bot to overcome all the hindrances put in front of it in order to stand above the rest.


ROBO SOCCOR :

1 v 1 football match between the robots.
