About us

Robotics innovation Result

IIIT Allahabad, an Institute of Excellence in the field of IT has grown leaps and bounds over the past few years.It has proved its mettle not only in academics but also in the overall development of its students through various fests! Aparoksha, is the annual technical festival of IIIT- Allahabad. It is a collaboration of tech-enthusiasts to showcase their creativity through technology.It organises a plethora of events and brings an opportunity for the students coming in to participate from all around the country to prove their calibre in science and technology.


So, to all the tech-enthusiasts who want their code to walk the talk, Topbot 3.0 is here for you! Come in, code, build, innovate and grab hold of the various prizes up for grabs.

TopBot is the flagship robotics event of Aparoksha, catering to the hunger of robogeeks and challenging your robot to accomplish a particular task and reach the finish line first !
Topbot gives you two captivating themes this year :

Bolt :

an obstacle avoiding contest that requires your bot to overcome all the hindrances put in front of it in order to stand above the rest.

Robo Soccer :

1v1 football match between the robots.

General Rules & Guidlines

Topbot will consist of two parallel themes ROBO Soccer and Bolt (one team can participate in both of them). The event will comprise two rounds:
1. Abstract submission (online)
2. Finals (onsite)

Registration

1. Prospective participants for Topbot need to register for event through the register page on the portal.
2.Entry Fee
3. The registration will be considered complete when the team leader receives a confirmation mail from team Topbot.

General Rules

1. Each team must assign a team leader.
2. A team may consist of a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 4 members.
3. Once a team is called to perform, they must report within 5 minutes.
4. Humans that cause deliberate interference with robots or damage to the field will be disqualified. It is expected that the aim of all teams is to play a fair and clean game.
5. The bot must not damage the arena or other bots.
6. Any attempt to bribe, influence or intimidate the judges or organisers will lead to immediate cancellation of contention.
7. The jury’s decision will be final.

Abstract Submission

Abstract Submission is the ONLINE qualifier round. The abstract must be submitted in PDF format. 1. The participant’s interpretation of the question.
2. Engineering theory and principle behind the question.
3. The participant’s approach.
4. Logic / Algorithm used in the solution.
5. Particular ingenuities of the proposed solution.
(Rules for abstract submission for both the themes available. )

Final Round

1. The final round will be held during Aparoksha at IIIT-Allahabad (18th March,2018 - 20th March,2018). The exact date would be notified later.
2. The participating teams will be required to bring their own robot kits and components and they have to prepare their vehicular robots in a time span of 48 hours after which the actual competition will be held.
3. The rules, specifications and marking scheme for each theme is given in its problem statement.
