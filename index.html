ABOUT US

TopBot is the multi-themed flagship robotics event of Aparoksha, IIIT-Allahabad . We cater to the hunger of robogeeks and challenge your robot to accomplish a particular task and reach the finish line first. We encourage you express your express yourself through your robots. So, to all the tech-enthusiasts who want their code to walk the talk, Topbot 3.0 is here for you! Come in and grab hold of the various prizes up for grabs. Code. Build. Innovate.

TOPBOT GAME ZONE

In this edition, Topbot will be having a gamezone where people who are not participating in the competition will get to play Robosoccer matches in the arena. Readymade robots will be provided and you have to only focus on winning by out-scoring the opposition. The winner of each match will get some exciting goodies and a few lucky winners will get a Topbot tee. Are you ready to walk the talk and throw your opponents of their perch ? Come join us in this battle of brains, brawns and balls.

GENERAL RULES AND GUIDELINES

1. A team may consist of a minimum of 1 members and a maximum of 3 members. Each team should have a team leader.

2. Teams are allowed to participate in both the themes.

3. Teams causing deliberate interference with robots or damage to the arena or opponent’s robot will be disqualified. It is expected that the aim of all teams is to play a fair and clean game.

4. Any attempt to bribe, influence or intimidate the judges or organisers will lead to immediate cancellation of contention.

5. The organisers’ decision will be final.


EVENTS

BOLT

An obstacle avoider event, where the participants have to design a heavy duty all terrain vehicular robot to tackle all the obstacles put in front of it to come out on top.

ROBO SOCCOR

2 vs 2 battle between robots where the participants will have to play the beautiful game by designing their own robotic versions of Ronaldo and Messi.

MAZE SOLVER

In this event competitor has to design a bot which will solve the maze with no prior knowledge of the maze by using different artifical intelligence algorithms.
